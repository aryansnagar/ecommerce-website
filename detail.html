Return Policy

Due to the nature of live animal sales, returns are generally not accepted. However, if there is a verified health issue within 72 hours of delivery, we offer replacements or store credit, based on veterinary documentation. We do not accept returns based on change of mind.

Terms and Conditions

We reserve the right to cancel any order that violates local animal welfare laws or international regulations. Please review our Terms and Conditions for full details.
